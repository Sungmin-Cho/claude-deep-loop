1. **acquire recovery liveness / corroborated — authentication-only public read rejects the marker it must authenticate**

     public reader and Task 10D marker-backed retry.
   - **Location**: plan `10905-10910`, `18556-18570`, and `19947-19985`.
   - **Evidence**: `readVerifiedState` throws `ANCHORED_TRANSACTION_PENDING` before its `fenceCheck`
     whenever the crash marker exists. `acquireAppTask` calls that public reader before observation
     parsing and before `withAppMutation`; therefore an exact retry never reaches the only gateway
     that can match caller+intent, recover the transaction, and project `already-acquired`.
   - **Required response**: add a marker-aware authentication-only helper. With no marker it must
     verify the canonical snapshot and identity; with a marker it must validate the recorded caller
     and authenticate owner/generation/runtime against an authenticated pre-mutation snapshot without
     recovery. Only then may acquire parse/path-normalize the observation and enter the intent-aware
     gateway that performs exact recovery and final re-fencing.
